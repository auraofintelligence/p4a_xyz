Clip brief

The everyday gripe

Someone says what plenty of workers already feel: the old career plan is wobbling. The clip avoids panic and points to the practical move: train wider, rotate through useful sectors, protect recovery time, and treat human adaptability as national infrastructure.

Description

BaitAI disruption lands first as job anxiety, retraining fatigue and a sense that the old advice no longer fits.
SwitchTry Everything Once turns scattered experience into general intelligence across real systems.
SystemIntermittent retirement, civic reserve pathways and health readiness reduce burnout before the next shock.
