What this page does

It Started as a Joke on a Joint

The first doorway should not feel like a lecture. It should feel like somebody on Straddie saying the quiet part out loud, then revealing there is a full constitutional machinery room behind the gag.

This page will carry the satire, the origin story, merch weirdness, and the core promise: we can laugh without becoming unserious.

Core modules

Kenny/Castle archetypeStart earthy and familiar: the ordinary Australian who can smell bullshit before the policy paper explains why.
Pee for Australia as signalThe toilet gag is the low-friction doorway: if politics is taking the piss, make the piss joke point somewhere useful.
Humour as an open invitationThe joke lowers the drawbridge, then the rabbit hole reveals the serious civic machinery underneath.

Origin rule

Keep it funny, then make it useful. The gag works only if it helps people move from disgust to agency without pretending the disgust was irrational.
