Clinical seriousness underneath

Start with care, not spectacle.

The grounded use case is pre-dementia and early dementia support: person-centred memory work, digital memoirs, carer support, behaviour patterns, quality-of-life signals and reports clinicians can actually read.

Hard guardrail

Aura Genesis is a provocation, research direction and story portal. Any health pathway would need qualified clinicians, ethics approval, privacy-by-design, validated outcomes and conservative language. Any candidate pathway would need anti-discrimination safeguards, consent and democratic legitimacy before it touched real preselection.
