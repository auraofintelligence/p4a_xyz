What this page does

Civic Surges

A movement cannot live on outrage alone. Civic Surges turn participation into timed missions: prepare, map, repair, learn, rest, repeat.

This page should eventually become interactive: choose your suburb, pick a mission, see the national pulse.

Core modules

Major and minor surgesShort, timed civic missions can map food waste, heat risk, local hazards, public assets and community needs without permanent campaign exhaustion.
Civic ReserveOlympic-scale volunteer energy can become a standing skills reserve for disaster response, local repair, sensor checks and public learning.
No burnout politicsThe rhythm is surge, rest, learn, rotate. Participation should build capacity, not chew through the same exhausted volunteers.

Participation loop

A Civic Surge turns big reform into doable missions: scan the problem, verify the signal, act locally, log the contribution, earn recognition, then pause before the next round.
