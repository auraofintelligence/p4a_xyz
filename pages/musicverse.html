11 / The album-world

Musicverse

A Protopian Gambit is the cultural layer where P4A stops being a policy stack and starts behaving like a playable myth: funny, wounded, civic, cosmic, and useful.

Enter the albumi C. infinity

Album signal

A Protopian Gambit

Produced as i C. infinity. This is not one anthem doing all the work. It is a track-by-track mythology for P4A: taking the piss, holding grief, building consent, valuing care, repairing systems, and making the future feel like something people can sing before they can legislate.
