What this page does

Deployment Gear

Gear is not just merch. It is a low-friction social object: a way to ask what P4A means without opening a policy paper.

This page keeps the rabbit hole physical, funny and visible in everyday life.

Core modules

Less splash, more classThe gear should be cheeky without being disposable: clean enough for public life, strange enough to start the conversation.
P4A merch purplePurple makes the movement instantly recognisable: playful, civic, electronic, local, and hard to confuse with standard party colours.
Humour with utilityObjects should do more than advertise: QR trails, event prompts, civic missions, local fundraisers and practical campaign tools.

Street interface

Deployment gear is the offline user interface for the rabbit hole. A shirt, mat, sticker or flag should let someone ask the first question before they are ready for the full policy stack.
