Insurance shouldn't eat the future.

A household-budget clip about premiums, risk and the obvious question: what can communities lawfully pool, prevent and fund before everything becomes unaffordable?

The everyday gripe

Someone stares at the wall and says the quiet part: if premiums keep rising, prevention has to become cheaper than panic. The clip opens into community mutuals, legal guardrails, reinsurance, risk reduction and L2 wealth funds backed by real assets.
